About Us

We are a small, passionate team with a deep love for wellness, ergonomic living, and creating products that make a real difference. What started with one simple idea — a smarter way to sit — has grown into a full collection of precision-engineered comfort cushions designed to elevate your daily life effortlessly.

At SeatSoother, we believe that comfort should be part of your design, not an afterthought. Everything we create is made to help you recover from pain, support your posture, and feel better in every seat — whether that's your office chair, car seat, or sofa at home.

Our flagship donut cushion was developed alongside physiotherapists and colorectal specialists to ensure it genuinely works. Every dimension, every foam density, and every cutout angle was engineered with one purpose: to eliminate pressure on sensitive anatomy so you can sit freely and heal faster.

Whether you’re managing hemorrhoids, recovering from surgery, or simply tired of back and tailbone pain — we’re here to help you build a life that’s more comfortable, more mobile, and uniquely yours.

Thank you for being here and supporting our small business.
